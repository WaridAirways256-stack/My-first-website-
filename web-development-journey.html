My Journey Into Web Development

Technology has always been interesting to me because it gives people the ability to create, solve problems, and share ideas with the world. My journey into web development started with curiosity about how websites are designed and how they work.

Where It Started

As I continued learning ICT, I became interested in understanding the technology behind websites. I discovered that websites are built using different technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Learning The Basics

The first step was learning HTML, which creates the structure of a website. After that, I started learning CSS to improve designs, layouts, colors, and user experience.

JavaScript introduced me to making websites interactive by adding animations, buttons, and dynamic features.

Challenges I Faced

Learning technology requires patience and practice. Some challenges include understanding new concepts, fixing errors, and finding better ways to solve problems.

What I Am Building

Through WaridAirways Tech Hub, I am documenting my progress, sharing technology knowledge, and creating projects that improve my skills.

My Future Goals

My goal is to continue developing my skills in software development, artificial intelligence, and digital innovation while creating useful technology solutions.

Conclusion

Learning web development is a continuous journey. Every project, mistake, and discovery helps me become better and understand the technology world more deeply.
